1. The folder includes the source codes of continuous-time beam tracking based on neural ordinary differential equation (ODE).
2. The folder is free for academic use, including dataset utilization, simulation result reproduction, model improvement, etc.
3. Due to storage restriction of Github, the dataset can be downloaded from:
Tsinghua Cloud: https://cloud.tsinghua.edu.cn/d/b1cf9fd7e09d466d94a5/
The final-version trained models can be downloaded from:
Tsinghua Cloud: https://cloud.tsinghua.edu.cn/d/3cdd6581990142939d3b/
4. For academic use, the related work may be published in:
Ke Ma, Fan Zhang, Wenqiang Tian, Zhaocheng Wang, ``Continuous-Time mmWave Beam Prediction with ODE-LSTM Learning Architecture'', minor revision, IEEE WCL.
